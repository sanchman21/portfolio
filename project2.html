CAMP+

Project


A campus management app

CAMP+ is like my child. What started as a 4th semester course project has turned into a startup. CAMP+ is a campus management app that manages day-to-day activities that happen at a residential univeristy campus. It is run by 4 Co-founders and 4 full-time employees.

Our aim was to ease some activities that happen at a college, to help students with some problems that they face. Only a student can understand what issues students face at a residential campus. Here are some of the features provided by CAMP+ App:

1. Event Management

2. Gate Entry/Exit Management

3. Complaints Management

CAMP+ is still going strong, and has made itself noticeable around the globe. It had received a grant by New-Gen IEDC, IIIT Allahabad, in August 20201. Here are some achievements of CAMP+ app

1. Semi-finalists at LA Tech-Stars

2. Selected for Step 2022 - Dubai
